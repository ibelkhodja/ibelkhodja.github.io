About Me

I am a rising junior majoring in Computer Science and Astronomy at Mount Holyoke College in South Hadley, Massachusetts.

By being involved in different aspects of my college, I gained a strong interest for a tight-knit community. I am an RA, the co-President of Computer Science Society, a data research assistant in the Astronomy Lab, a Reunion worker, and a Physics TA/Grader.

When I am not studying or planning events, I like to read and watch mysteries or thrillers, or go outside for a walk.

Communication is important, but understanding another person's idea is key. Born in France but originally from Algeria, I lived in 5 different countries. My experience helped me open my eyes to various cultures and how people express their ideas differently.
